# Santa Blast Factory

**Santa Blast factory** is a game where you play as a hero on a mission to save Santa Claus before Christmas. The goal is to navigate through a maze, destroy enemies using bombs, and uncover hidden power-ups in destructible tiles.

## Features:
- Use bombs to destroy enemies and obstacles.
- Bombs will blast after 3 seconds.
- Hidden power-ups within destructible tiles they should be collected.
- Save Santa and he will reward you with some extra time
- Find the exit under the tiles, after defeating all enemies and saving Santa the Exit will be unlocked.
- on the screen track your score, number of bombs, your concurrent bombs, blast radius, number of enemies left on the map, the time left and your lives.
- you can mute and unmute the game
- you can start new game or pause and continue your game

## POWERUPS:
You need to collect the Powerups from the tiles
- You can get up to 2 extra lives, track your lives on the heart counter on the bottom left of the map
- you can get 5 extra bombs, unlimited
- you can increase your speed twice 
- you can increase your Blast Radius up to 8 tiles
- you can increase your concurrent bombs up to 8 bombs

## POWERDOWNS: 
Powerdowns are collected automatically when they are revealed; and only once
- you can get 5 less bombs
- 3 more enemies could appear on the map
## How to Play:
- Move through the map, placing bombs to defeat enemies.
- Look out for hidden power-ups to boost your abilities.
- Save Santa to gain more time. free him from his enclosure of tiles by destroying them. 
- Remember to save Santa and kill the Grinch enemies before time left until christmas runs out or you will lose.
